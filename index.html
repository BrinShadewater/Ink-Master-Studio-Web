Print-ready DTG and DTF artwork in one guided studio.

InkMaster Studio helps print shops turn uploaded artwork into production-ready files, apparel mockups, customer proofs, and handoff packages without forcing operators through a wall of technical controls.

The workflow covers local artwork intake, DTG and DTF preflight checks, print recipe selection, cleanup support, placement review, proof approval, and export-ready production packages.

  • Preflight: check resolution, transparency, placement, and production risk before export.
  • Proof: prepare mockups and approval details that customers can review clearly.
  • Handoff: package artwork, settings, notes, and print-shop files for repeatable output.
